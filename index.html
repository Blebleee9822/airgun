Welcome to our site

This is a basic introduction to Halifax Airgun Club.

There are no age restrictions as long as minors are accompanied by an adult.

No need to book. Just walk in.

Our Ranges

Whether you have your own Airgun or would like to try one of ours, our indoor specially created ranges cater for the novice to expert alike.

We have 2 floors of dedicated target shooting, which vary from 11 lanes of 10m hand cranked targets, 1 lane of 16m with electric target retrieval. 3 lanes of 39M, 2 lanes of 35m and 2 lanes of 30M. Lots of spinning and resetting targets

We are a non membership "club" where you pay as you go for lane hire by the time spent here.

Facilities

If you get thirsty or peckish, we have hot and cold drinks and snacks available.

We feel that this is a unique attraction in the area which provides the Airgun owners of Calderdale and the surrounding areas with a dedicated indoor range, making target shooting an all weather all year round experience.

We cater for the individual or groups and can offer corporate packages.

Technical Services

We also have an onsite chronograph to enable us to test any gun to make sure it conforms to the current laws.

We also have a compressor for filling PCP dive bottles, have your bottle filled while you shoot.

We hold a current RFD license, which enables us to sell air rifles/pistols and all things related.

Our on site shop is open for business. Pay us a visit
